Load – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Data We Collect

• Email address (only when you sign in with Apple, Google, or email)
• Workout logs, bodyweight, and strength data you enter
• HealthKit data (body weight & workouts) — only if you explicitly grant permission
• Anonymous analytics (which screens you use, crashes)

2. How We Use It

We only use your data to power the app — show your progress, calculate 1RMs, generate the Hulkameter, and sync across your devices via Supabase.

3. Third Parties

• Supabase (EU-hosted database & auth) — GDPR compliant
• Apple Sign In & Google Sign In — their privacy policies apply
• No ads, no tracking, no selling data ever.

4. HealthKit

We read body weight and write workouts only with your explicit permission. You can revoke access anytime in Settings → Health → Load.

5. Contact

Questions? Email: load.app.privacy@gmail.com

By using Load you agree to this policy. We’ll never be evil.
